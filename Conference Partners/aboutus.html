Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion Statement

At Conference Partners, Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI) are at the very core of everything we do, because we believe in building and hosting events that bring diverse people together. We understand that the more diverse the group of attendees and stakeholders, the more innovative the solutions and the broader the perspectives that emerge, leading to a richer event outcome and impact.

We strive to build exceptional event experiences that are inclusive for everyone and are accessible and welcoming to diverse attendees. We put this into practice in our everyday, from our workshop to event planning.

We advocate for:

1

Diversity in representation

We believe in a workforce and events that reflect the communities we serve.

2

Accessibility for everyone

We are committed to providing accessible experiences for everyone, including those with disabilities.

3

Equity in opportunity

We believe in equal pay, no gender bias, and equal opportunities for growth within the company, fostering an environment where everyone feels welcome, valued, and empowered to do their best work.
